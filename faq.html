How do clear aligners straighten teeth?

Aligners (invisible braces) gradually shift teeth into the ideal position, making your teeth straight. This is achieved through wearing a series of aligners, each for at least 10 days at a time, which move the teeth up to 0.3mm per aligner until the ideal alignment has been reached. There are key differences between conventional braces and aligners.

Braces apply a pull force that gradually shift teeth into place, and aligners use a push force to rotate your teeth. Each aligner you will be wearing, makes a smooth, very mild teeth rotation.

How to know if I am suitable for clear aligners treatment?

Every Clear Aligner Journey begins with a Smile Assessment. Our Smile Consultant will then contact you to get 6 photos of your teeth from different angles. Then, the orthodontist will be able to determine if you are a fit candidate of clear aligners.

What if I still have my wisdom teeth?

In most instances, the presence of wisdom teeth will not obstruct treatment progress. Before fitting your clear aligners, your orthodontist will perform a 3D Intraoral Scan or X-ray examination to assess whether the wisdom tooth would lead to potential problems later down the line.

Even if the wisdom teeth are erupted and to be extracted during clear aligner treatment, it shouldn’t affect progress in most cases: your orthodontist can trim the aligner back and leave space to accommodate the erupted tooth or surgical site.

Does tooth extraction really necessary before aligners treatment?

Not all cases will require extractions - in fact, it is usually not necessary. If it’s possible to align all existing teeth into a healthy bite and correct positioning, then they will do it without extractions. Again, it’s best to consult the dentist and orthodontist so they can design the best solution for your treatment.

Why do I need IPR?

IPR (Interproximal Reduction), is the process of creating tiny amounts of space between pairs of teeth in order to allow them to move as needed during treatment. If your teeth are crowded, there may not be sufficient space for your teeth to move into their desired position.

IPR is usually preferred over teeth extraction because your dentist has more control over the amount of space created. IPR only shaves off between 0.2 to 0.3 millimetres of your enamel. However, if a substantial amount of space is needed, your dentist may recommend teeth extraction instead.

What if I have missing teeth?

Each individual’s smile is assessed by our orthodontists on a case by case basis. We will review your smile and assess your eligibility for clear aligners treatment.

What is attachment and why do I need it?

Aligners attachments are little, tooth-colored dots made of composite filling material that are bonded to teeth to help the aligners move teeth. Attachments serve as anchors for your aligners, allowing the aligners to fit more snugly against your teeth and move more effectively. When your teeth shift into alignment the way they’re intended, your treatment with aligners will proceed smoothly, and you’ll have perfectly straight teeth before you know it.

  • 5) Receive your aligners!
What do I expect post-treatment?

Protect your new smile! We strongly recommend you safeguard your new smile with our custom-fit retainers.

Without retainers, teeth have a tendency to shift back to their original positions. Keep them from moving back with our custom-fit retainers right after you complete your clear aligners treatment!

How to preserve my new smile after treatment?

Wear your retainers for 22 hours a day (except for eating and drinking) for the first two weeks. After that, you should wear your retainers only while you sleep.

We recommend replacing your retainers every year to keep them fresh :)

What should I take note of during aligners treatment
  • 1) You’re required to wear your aligners at least 20 - 22 hours a day.
  • 2) You’re required to change to a new set every 10-15 days.
  • 3) Make sure you remove your aligners when you eat and drink (except water).
  • 4) Avoid cleaning your aligners with toothpaste & mouthwash.
